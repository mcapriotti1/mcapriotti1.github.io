QUBO to Maxcut (Associated with Los Alamos National Laboratory)

GitHub, arXiv

Collaborated on authoring a research paper on quantum algorithms by applying the Quantum Approximate Optimization Algorithm (QAOA) to solve complex combinatorial optimization problems. Designed and implemented “warm-start” strategies that integrate classical optimization with quantum computation, enabling faster and more accurate solutions for unconstrained problems at lower circuit depths. Extended these techniques to a variety of problem types—including portfolio optimization (simulated using Brownian Motion), travelling salesman, and maximum independent set—by transforming QUBO problems into Max-Cut instances.

Tech Used: Qiskit, Matplotlib, Python, Jupyter Notebook
